Blue

Blue is the color fo the sky and sea. It is often associated with depth and stability. It symbolizes trust, loyalty wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith, truth, and heaven.

Green

Green is the color of nature. It symbolizes growth, harmony, freshness, and fertility. Green has strong emotional correspondence with safety.

Red

Red is the color fo fire and blood, so it is associated with energy, war, determination as well as passion, desire, and love.
